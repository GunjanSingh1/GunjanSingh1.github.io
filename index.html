About

I am a Ph.D. scholar at the Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Delhi. My academic advisor is Dr. Raghava Mutharaju (IIIT, Delhi) and I am also working closely with Dr. Sumit Bhatia (IBM Research AI, India). I have been awarded with the prestigious IBM PhD Fellowship for the year 2020. My research interests include Knowledge Graphs, Ontology Reasoning, Ontology Benchmarking, Reinforcement Learning, Deep Learning and Description Logics. Specifically I am working on developing non conventional methods for efficient reasoning over expressive ontology languages such as OWL 2.

Prior to joining my Ph.D. I worked as an Assistant Professor at Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology (A. K. T. U) for 2.5 years. I completed my Masters in Computer Science and Engineering with two gold-medals from Aligarh Muslim University (A. M. U), Aligarh, India, 2016 and my Bachelors in Computer Engineering from Aligarh Muslim University (A. M. U), Aligarh, India, 2014.
