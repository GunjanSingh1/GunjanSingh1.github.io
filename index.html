About

I am a Ph. D. student at the Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Delhi and also an IBM Research Fellow. My academic advisor is Dr. Raghava Mutharaju (IIIT, Delhi). I am also working in close collaboration with Dr. Sumit Bhatia (Adobe, India).

My research interests include Knowledge Graphs, Ontology Reasoning, Ontology Benchmarking, Reinforcement Learning, Deep Learning, and Description Logics. Specifically, I am working on developing non-conventional methods for efficient reasoning over expressive ontology languages such as OWL 2.

Prior to joining my Ph. D., I worked as an Assistant Professor at Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology (A. K. T. U) for 2.5 years. I completed my Masters in Computer Science and Engineering with two gold-medals from Aligarh Muslim University (A. M. U), India, 2016 and Bachelors in Computer Engineering from Aligarh Muslim University (A. M. U), India, 2014.
